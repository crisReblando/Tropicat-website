About TropiCat Bites

🎯 Vision

To become a trusted and leading online pet brand in the Philippines by providing healthy, sustainable, and high-quality cat treats while promoting responsible pet ownership and animal welfare.

⭐ Mission

Tropicat is committed to providing high-quality cat essentials through a convenient and engaging e-commerce platform. We aim to deliver excellent customer service, promote responsible pet care, and create a trusted community for cat lovers by offering reliable products, fast delivery, and a seamless online shopping experience.

🐱 Business Description

TropiCat Bites are oven-baked, high-protein treats prepared using fresh chicken and tuna. Combined with organic components such as taurine, omega oils, and malunggay to satisfy nutritional requirements of domestic cats in tropical regions.

🏢 Business Structure

Partners: Dela Cruz, Gonzales, Llames, Reblando

Business Objectives: TropiCat Bites aims for immediate and long-term goals, focusing on expansion, social impact, and brand recognition. The company plans to sell 1,500 units in the first three months and establish distribution agreements in Metro Manila to gain market traction. Advocacy initiatives for animal welfare and ethical pet care are also planned, along with national growth over the next two to three years, introducing new products like dental treats and kitten recipes. TropiCat Bites aspires to become a leading local premium cat treat brand in the Philippines through advocacy-driven branding and expanded distribution.

About Us

TropiCat Bites is a proudly Filipino brand creating premium cat treats made with natural tropical ingredients.

Our goal is simple: give cats healthier, tastier, and safer treats while supporting animal welfare across the Philippines.

Our Mission

To create high-quality, nutritious cat treats while helping rescued and stray cats in need. Every purchase helps support shelters and feeding programs.
